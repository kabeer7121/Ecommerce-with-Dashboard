FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you deliver all over Pakistan?

Yes! We deliver to all major cities and towns across Pakistan including Karachi, Islamabad, Peshawar, Quetta, and more.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 working days. Karachi orders may arrive within 2–3 days.

Can I exchange a product?

Yes, we offer a 7-day exchange policy. The item must be unused, with tags intact. Contact us and we'll arrange a pickup.

How does Cash on Delivery work?

Simply place your order, and our delivery team will bring it to your door. You pay in cash when you receive your package — no advance payment needed!
